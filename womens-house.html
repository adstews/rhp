Women's House

A spacious coastal home in Madison, CT — designed for women building lives of purpose in recovery.

The Home

6,300 Sq Ft of Comfort & Community

Our Women's House is a former inn set in the heart of Madison, Connecticut. The elegant, spacious home accommodates up to 14 women in private and shared rooms with private baths. With four fireplaces, three lounge areas, a chef's kitchen, a garden, and outdoor lounge space — it's a place that feels like home from day one.

The house is walkable to the beach, downtown Madison, 12-step meetings, and the train station. It's also across the street from the bus stop, making it easy to get to work, therapy, or anywhere you need to be.

Recovery Program

Built Around Connection & Purpose

📚

In-House Meetings & Workshops

The Women's House hosts its own 12-step meeting and opens its doors every Wednesday evening for a Women's Speaker Meeting with the local AA community. Morning recovery workshops for those not at work or IOP.

🤝

Recovery Coaching

A live-in Recovery Coach provides an elevated level of support — professional oversight, accountability, and one-on-one guidance to help you build your personalized recovery plan.

🍴

Shared Dinners & Community

Rotating dinner assignments, daily check-ins, peer-led workshops, and weekend outings create a rhythm of belonging and sisterhood that lasts well beyond your stay.

🐎

Wrap-Around Clinical Support

Equine therapy, neurofeedback, EMDR, gym membership, yoga, sound retreats — we coordinate clinical resources that lift up every aspect of your life: physical, emotional, and social.

Location

Madison, Connecticut

A Walkable, Coastal Community

Madison is a charming shoreline town with a tight-knit AA and NA community. The house is a 15-minute walk to the train station with service to New York, New Haven, Providence, and Boston. Walk to the beach, downtown shops, meetings, and clinical support — everything you need is close by.
