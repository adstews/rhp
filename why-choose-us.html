What Makes Us Different

Beyond a Typical Sober House

Owner-Operated

When you call Right Path, you get Lisa or Rob — not a call center. We are personally involved in every resident's recovery journey.

🧠

Wrap-Around Clinical Support

Most Level II programs only encourage outside clinical access. We actively coordinate neurofeedback, EMDR, equine therapy, IOP, and trauma-specialized therapists.

💪

Our staff is trained in de-escalation, boundary setting, and trauma-informed care — creating a safe environment that prioritizes respect and dignity.

Beautiful Coastal Location

Trusted Partnerships

Our Approach

Structure That Transforms

Recovery Is the Whole Person

We don't just encourage sobriety — we cultivate belonging, accountability, and embodied transformation. From shared dinners to gym memberships, from therapy requirements to 12-step work, everything is designed to help you build a life worth living.

Everyone Is In Recovery

At Right Path, staff and residents alike are required to be engaged with an experienced therapist and 12-step sponsor. We don't ask you to do anything we aren't doing ourselves.
