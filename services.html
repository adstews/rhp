Our Services

A holistic approach to recovery — clinical support, wellness, community, and purpose.

Recovery Services

What We Provide

📚

12-Step Workshops & Meetings

8–10 hours per week of in-house workshops and meetings, plus 5 hours of outside community 12-step meetings. We also host a weekly Women's Speaker Meeting every Wednesday evening.

Included
🧠

Neurofeedback

Partnered with Gray Matters of Westport, we offer on-site neurofeedback to help restore the brain's natural balance. It reduces cravings, improves impulse control, and enhances emotional resilience.

On-Site
💡

EMDR & Therapy

All residents participate in therapy throughout their stay. We provide dedicated telehealth space and connect residents with nearby trauma specialists and EMDR providers.

Required
🐎

Equine Therapy

Devine Equine offers three programs — therapy, therapeutic groundwork, and mounted riding lessons — all available at cost to our residents.

At Cost
💪

Fitness & Wellness

Free gym membership at Coastal Fitness including sauna, yoga, Pilates, barre, and core-strength classes. Bikes and kayaks available to enjoy the shoreline.

Included
🤝

Case Management & Coaching

Strong initial case management with weekly check-ins. We help you set up clinical plans, find employment, and build the skills to manage your own recovery long-term.

Included
