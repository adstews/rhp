About Right Path House

Founded on personal experience. Driven by purpose. Dedicated to lasting recovery.

Our Mission

Recovery Beyond Sobriety

What We Believe

Recovery is defined not only by physical sobriety but by an improved quality of life. Every person who walks through our doors is offered the opportunity to create a new recovery lifestyle — one built on belonging, accountability, and transformation.

Our Story

Why Right Path Exists

Right Path House was founded in memory of MacKenzie Morley, who passed away at 27 from liver failure arising from addiction. She couldn't find a sober house that could accommodate her life and her recovery at a price she could afford. She left behind her 8-year-old son, Brodie, and a loving family.

Co-founder Lisa Ferguson, decades sober herself, had seen the benefits of sober living firsthand. She opened Right Path as a blend of affordability and quality — a gently structured sober living house that was reasonably priced, where real recovery could take root.
